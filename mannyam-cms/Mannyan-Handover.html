1. Accessing the System

The MANNYAM CMS is securely protected. Only authorized users can access the administrative sections.

  • Navigate to /login on your website.
  • Enter your registered Admin email and password.
  • Once logged in, navigate to /settings or any of the admin modules (e.g., /settings/users).
Important: If you are logged in as a "Customer", you will be redirected to the Traveller Dashboard and denied access to CMS operations. Ensure your account is upgraded to "Admin" or "Content Manager".

2. Content Management

The CMS provides an intuitive interface for writing journal posts, creating landing pages, and building luxury journey packages.

Journal Posts (Blog)

  • Go to CMS > Journal.
  • Click New Post to start writing.
  • You can use standard Markdown to format your text (e.g., **bold**, # Heading).
  • You can add a cover image by pasting a URL from the Media Library.
  • Click Publish to make it live instantly.

Journeys & Experiences (Packages)

  • Go to CMS > Packages.
  • Here you define the bespoke journeys available for checkout.
  • Set the Base Amount and the Deposit Amount required to book.
  • Ensure you assign beautiful cover imagery.

3. Media Library

The Media Library stores all your images securely in the cloud.

  • Go to CMS > Media Library.
  • Drag and drop high-quality images to upload them.
  • Once uploaded, you can click the "Copy Link" button to get the URL.
  • Paste this URL into any Cover Image field for Posts, Pages, or Packages.

4. Booking & Customer Operations

When a client checks out securely via Razorpay, a Booking is generated.

  • Go to Bookings to view all reservations.
  • Status Meanings:
    • Pending: Customer started checkout but hasn't paid yet.
    • Confirmed: Customer paid the deposit.
    • Paid: Customer paid the full amount.
  • Refunding: If you need to refund a customer, you can click the "Refund" button on a Confirmed or Paid booking. Enter the amount to process a partial or full refund directly back to their original payment method via Razorpay.
Note on Balances: Customers can log into their own portal at /account to pay their outstanding balance securely. The system handles this automatically.

5. System Settings & Users

System settings should only be modified by trusted Administrators.

Payment Gateways

  • Go to Settings > Payment Gateways.
  • Here you can update your Razorpay Key ID, Razorpay Key Secret, and Webhook Secret.
  • These are securely encrypted and stored in your database.

Users & Team

  • Go to Settings > Users & Team.
  • You will see a list of every user registered on your site.
  • You can change a user's role using the dropdown menu.
  • Roles:
    • Admin: Full access to everything, including settings and refunds.
    • Content Manager: Can edit posts and pages, but cannot view bookings or settings.
    • Customer: Can only view their own bookings in the client portal.
